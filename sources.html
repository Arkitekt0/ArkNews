Journalism Ethics & Professional Standards

  • Accuracy: Verify information before publishing. Cross‑check facts with multiple credible sources.
  • Independence: Avoid conflicts of interest and maintain editorial separation from sponsors or political groups.
  • Accountability: Correct mistakes promptly and transparently.
  • Fairness: Represent subjects honestly without distortion or selective omission.
  • Transparency: Disclose methods, sourcing limitations, and potential biases when relevant.
  • Minimize Harm: Consider the impact of reporting on vulnerable individuals and communities.
  • No Plagiarism: Attribute all quotes, data, and reporting to their original sources.
